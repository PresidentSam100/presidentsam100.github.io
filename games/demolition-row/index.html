Demolition Row 🧱

Plus-shaped blocks fall from the top. Move, rotate, and drop them so 4 or more same-colored blocks connect (up/down/left/right) — they demolish, the rest fall, and chains cascade. Clear the stack and don't let it reach the pink line!

Mode
Players
Each player — tap to switch Human/CPU; pick each CPU's difficulty
Match Format
Power-ups & blocks
⛓ Iron — clears two full vertical columns.
💎 Crystal — a colorless wildcard plus; press rotate to cycle it to a color, then drop it to join a match.
⚡ Thunder — erases every block of one color, then cascades.
🐌 Slow — slows the fall speed for a while (single-player only).
★ Star — appears in Endless; demolish 3 to trigger the ×20 bonus period.
▦ Stone — obstacle; can't be color-matched, but cracks when adjacent blocks clear (breaks after 3 hits).
Demolish blocks to fill the gauge — a power-up is then awarded as the center of your next plus piece.
