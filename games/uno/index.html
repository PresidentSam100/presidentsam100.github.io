Match color, number, or symbol — empty your hand and call UNO!

New Game

These 3 wilds can be played on anything and let you pick the color. The rule is shown to everyone when played — enforce it yourselves (honor system).

How it plays here

You play against CPU opponents. Click a glowing card to play it, or draw from the deck. Includes Draw 2, Skip, Reverse, Wild, Wild Draw Four (with challenges), Wild Shuffle Hands, and your custom wilds. When a player hits one card, hit UNO! in time — or catch someone who forgets.
